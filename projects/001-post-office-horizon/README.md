# Post Office Horizon

**Project ID**: 001
**Created**: 2026-05-21
**Status**: Active

Enterprise architecture governance for the **Post Office Horizon replacement programme** — the procurement and delivery of a replacement for the Fujitsu-operated Horizon branch IT system at the centre of the Post Office scandal, including the exit from Fujitsu.

## Structure

- `external/` — external reference documents (org charts, governance structures, vendor materials)
- `decisions/` — Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)
- `diagrams/` — architecture diagrams
- `research/` — research artifacts

## Conventions

ArcKit artifacts follow the naming convention `ARC-001-{TYPE}-v{VERSION}.md`. Cross-project artifacts (e.g. architecture principles) live in `projects/000-global/`.
